Education Department Intern

Academic Peer Mentor

INST314

I had the opportunity to become an Academic Peer Mentor (AMP) in 2018, during my junior year of college as the University of Maryland. An Academic Peer Mentor is essentially a liaison between the students and the professor. I was the AMP for INST314: Statistics for Information Science for the Fall semester.

My responsibilities included:

  • Help students improve in their understanding of statistics
  • Assist the professor with finding online tools that would enhance learning outside of the classroom
  • Supported students through homework, assignments, or studying
